Motor A Forward: PB4 (PWM) HIGH, PC10 (DIR) LOW
Motor A Backward: PB4 (PWM) LOW, PC10 (DIR) HIGH (PWM)
Motor B Forward: PA7 (PWM) HIGH, PC11 (DIR) LOW  
Motor B Backward: PA7 (PWM) LOW, PC11 (DIR) HIGH (PWM)

 A-IB         →    PB4          Motor A PWM (TIM3_CH1)
 A-IA         →    PC10         Motor A Direction
 B-IB         →    PA7          Motor B PWM (TIM3_CH2)
 B-IA         →    PC11         Motor B Direction
VCC           →    Battery+     7.4V power
GND           →    STM32 GND    Common ground
